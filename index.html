Understanding Health Scores

Each menu item receives a health score from 0-100 based on:

  • Protein Content: High-protein items (chicken, salmon, eggs) score higher
  • Cooking Method: Grilled, baked, and steamed items score better than fried options
  • Nutritional Balance: Items with better macro and micronutrient profiles
